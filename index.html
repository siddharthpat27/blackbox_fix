宫保鸡丁 (Gōngbǎo Jīdīng) - Kung Pao Chicken

Ingredients: chicken, peanuts, vegetables, soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, Szechuan pepper, dried red chilies, garlic, ginger...

Instructions: Stir-fry the chicken and set aside. Stir-fry the peanuts and vegetables. Add the chicken back to the wok. Add the sauce and stir to combine. Serve over rice.
